Our history

Our story begins in 1980 along the streets of New York. It was where Efac brothers opened its first store. After Andrew Lewis joined the company in 1982, we expanded to Chicago and Vancouver, Canada and then on to California, Washington, D.C. and Seattle. By 1996, we would cross the Pacific to open our first store in Japan, followed by Europe in 1998 and China in 1999. Over the next two decades we would grow to one of the biggest coffeeshop franchise in the world
